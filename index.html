About Ho Ngoc Ha

Ho Ngoc Ha is a Vietnamese model, pop singer, actress and entertainer. She was born in An Cuu, Hue, then lived in Quang Binh. Her paternal grandfather was French. She studied piano at the Military College of Arts in Hanoi, where she started her modeling and acting career. She is now living in Ho Chi Minh City

Career - Albums - Awards

Debut album

    She came to Hanoi and met with musician Huy Tuan and discussed the implementation of her first album. After that, Huy Tuan joined her debut album's production, which was called "24 Hours 7 Days", released in 2004 by Viet Tan Studio. Songs included in the album are produced by Tuan and are composed by famous composers such as Duc Tri, Nguyen Xinh Xo, Hong Kien, Duong Thu, and An Hieu. In the album, the title song gained a lot of success and was performed by Ha on various music shows. Despite much investment, this album did not make much resonance for Hà because the album was not widely advertised and there was no promotional music video, until her nude "art" photos were posted online; then her name was said to be "floating like alcohol". In addition, that was when feelings emerged between Hà and musician Duc Tri.
